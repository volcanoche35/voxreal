# VoxReal

**Make it real. Make it count.**

VoxReal is a global online survey platform that empowers individuals and organizations to create, distribute, and analyze surveys with ease. Whether you're gathering customer feedback, conducting market research, or running academic studies — VoxReal makes every voice count.

## Overview

- **Create** — Design surveys with a powerful, intuitive builder
- **Distribute** — Reach respondents worldwide through multiple channels
- **Analyze** — Get real-time insights with rich visualizations
- **Scale** — From small polls to enterprise-grade research
